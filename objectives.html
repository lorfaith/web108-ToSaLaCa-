Objectives

  • Retrieve all near-expiry food items listed by grocery stores.
  • Filter available food items by location and category.
  • Track user roles and permissions (Role-Based Access Control)
  • Monitor food reservations made by customers or charities
  • View all donation calls from charities and store responses
  • Show real-time notifications for new food listings
  • Identify non-compliant items rejected by the system
  • List uploaded documents or photos per listing
  • Show history of food item expirations and usage
